What Personal Information We Collect

Personal Information refers to information about an identifiable individual. Urxion collects Personal Information when you voluntarily provide it through our Site or through other means. For example, we may collect Personal Information when you:

  • Contact us via email, telephone, mail, or other correspondence.
  • Register for a service we provide.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Utilize our AI-powered sales tools and services.

The Personal Information we may collect includes:

  • Basic Information: Your full name, contact details (such as address, telephone number, and email address).
  • Professional Information: Your job title, company name, and industry.
  • Usage Information: Details about how you use our services, including interactions with our AI tools.
  • Technical Information: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical data collected automatically.

How We Collect Information Through Technological Means

When you visit our Site, certain information is automatically collected by your web browser. This information may include:

  • Device Information: Your IP address, domain name, and numerical IP address.
  • Browser Information: Type of browser you use, pages you view, and files you request.
  • Usage Data: The time and date of your visit, the duration of your visit, and the pages you navigate.

We use this information to:

  • Improve the functionality and performance of our Site.
  • Understand how visitors use our Site to enhance user experience.
  • Personalize content and advertisements you see on our Site.

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

Urxion utilizes "cookies" and other similar technologies to enhance functionality and user experience. These may track information such as:

  • IP address
  • Type of web browser and operating system used
  • Pages visited on our Site
  • Interactions with our services and AI tools

We use this information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Analyze user behavior and preferences.
  • Deliver personalized content and advertisements.

If you wish to disable cookies, please refer to your browser's help menu for instructions on how to change or restrict them.

AI Workflow and Demo Data

When you use a URXION public demo or provide documents for a scoped workflow, we use those materials only to generate the requested RFP, compliance, SDR, or custom workflow output. Demo workspaces are temporary and are designed to be deleted after 48 hours.

URXION does not use your uploaded documents to train URXION-owned models. Some workflows may use third-party AI or infrastructure providers to process your request. For production engagements, provider usage, retention, access controls, and export requirements are scoped before implementation.

Public demo output is for evaluation only and does not replace qualified legal, compliance, procurement, or business review.
