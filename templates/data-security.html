Demo uploads

Public demo workspaces are temporary and designed to be deleted after 48 hours. Demo output is for evaluation only.

No URXION model training

Uploaded documents are not used to train URXION-owned models. They are used to generate the workflow output you request.

Human approval

URXION prepares review-ready artifacts. It does not auto-submit proposals, certify compliance, or send outbound messages on its own.

Third-party providers

Provider usage is disclosed before production.

Some workflows may use third-party AI or infrastructure providers to process your request. For production engagements, provider usage, data residency needs, retention, access controls, and deletion/export requirements are scoped before implementation.

Human access

Access should match the work.

URXION is founder-led today, so production access expectations are discussed directly. The goal is limited, purpose-bound access for setup, support, review, and troubleshooting.

Operating controls

Designed around cautious workflows.

Evidence first

Outputs should show requirements, sources, excerpts, gaps, or assumptions where possible.

Fail closed

Missing or unsupported information is flagged instead of guessed through.

Scoped retention

Production retention is agreed before implementation based on workflow, risk, and operational needs.

Deletion and export

Deletion/export expectations should be part of the production scope, not an afterthought.
