Send if available

  • Subcontractor package
  • WSIB clearance
  • Insurance certificate
  • Training records
  • Safety policy or site safety plan
  • Contract or start-work requirements
  • Project location and planned start date

Red flags URXION looks for

  • Expired WSIB
  • Insurance legal-name mismatch
  • Missing additional insured wording
  • Missing training records
  • Generic safety policy with no project-specific controls
  • Missing subcontractor contract evidence
