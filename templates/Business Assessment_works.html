About Sean:

Sean Brennan, Founder of Urxion

As a seasoned sales veteran with over two decades of experience, I've experienced and witnessed firsthand the struggles of startups and small businesses to generate leads and book those crucial first meetings.

My sales journey began in life insurance sales, where I learned from the school of hard knocks my skills forprospecting business owners and consistently exceeded sales targets. I led national sales teams at prominent companies, but I couldn't shake off the frustration I saw in talented people that lacked the expertise to effectively sell, or worse they were able to sell but were never able to teach and coach others to do it so they could focus on their business.

Having used and reviewed countless softphone products and CRM systems, I realized none truly addressed the core issues I observed. They lacked the intelligent guidance and support that new and seasoned sales professionals needed to consistently overcome objections and secure meetings.

This frustration sparked an idea - what if there was a service that combined the power of AI with the human touch of experienced sales agents?

Driven by my vision, I leveraged my extensive sales knowledge and network to develop Meeting Generator. I poured my years of experience into crafting a comprehensive training program for agents, ensuring they were equipped with the skills and confidence to excel in even the most challenging sales conversations.

I meticulously designed the AI-powered objection handling system, drawing upon my own successful strategies and incorporating best practices from the industry.

Meeting Generator was born from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by startups and a passion for empowering them with the tools and resources they need to succeed.

Today, Meeting Generator stands as a beacon of innovation in the sales landscape, helping startups and small businesses unlock their full potential by connecting them with the right clients and driving meaningful growth.
