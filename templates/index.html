No. Messy documents are normal. Better source material produces better output, but discovery can start with what you have.

No. URXION prepares review-ready work. Humans approve and submit.

No. Uploaded documents are not used to train URXION-owned models.

Can I start with one workflow?

Yes. That is the preferred path. Bring one workflow and we will tell you if it is worth automating.
