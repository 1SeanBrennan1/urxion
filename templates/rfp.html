Red flags

RFP red flags URXION looks for.

  • Mandatory forms hidden in appendices
  • Pricing format mismatch
  • Unsupported experience claims
  • Missing insurance or certification evidence
  • Evaluation criteria not mapped to sections
  • Addenda not incorporated
