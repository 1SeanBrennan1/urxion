Comparison

Public demo vs production workflow.

Public demo

  • Demo-level model route for fast preview
  • Cached public RFP listings or pasted text
  • TXT works best for compliance package text
  • PDF/DOCX extraction is best effort
  • Temporary demo workspace
  • Human review reminders but no configured approval workflow

Production URXION

  • Tailored model routing by task and risk
  • Full RFP package, attachments, addenda, Q&A, and evidence library
  • Richer document extraction and source previews
  • Requirement-level evidence matrix and audit trail
  • Scoped retention, access, and deletion requirements
  • Named human approval gates before final use

RFP workflows

Production uses the complete solicitation package plus your company evidence to create compliance matrices, gap lists, proposal drafts, and approval packets.

Compliance workflows

Production reviews source documents with stronger extraction, evidence previews, and qualified human approval instead of auto-certification.

Custom agents

Production scopes tools, data access, model tiers, approval gates, and evaluation tests around the actual business workflow.
