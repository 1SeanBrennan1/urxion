Let's analyze the sales activity needed to reach your profit goals for both internal and external sales models.

Important Note:

This analysis provides a simplified model for comparison purposes. In real-world scenarios:

  • Fixed costs often increase as sales volume grows (e.g., need for additional staff or infrastructure).
  • Cost per sale typically decreases with higher volumes due to economies of scale.
  • Variable costs may change based on order size or customer segments.

These factors can significantly impact actual profitability and break-even points. Consider consulting with a financial advisor for a more detailed analysis tailored to your specific business situation.
