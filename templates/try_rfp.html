Important demo limits

The public demo is intentionally simple.

  • Public opportunity matching uses cached listing snippets and sample fallbacks.
  • Many official RFPs require login, attachments, or buyer portals to access full documents.
  • You paste the actual RFP on the next page, right before generating the package.
  • Production workflows use stronger models, full RFP packages, source files, evidence folders, and deeper review.
  • No demo output should be used as a final bid without human review.
