Challenger

Chapters

  • Chapter 1: The Evolving Journey of Solution Selling

    • Delves into the rise of solution selling to address product commoditization.
    • Discusses how solution selling creates complexity and challenges.
    • Highlights the changing landscape of customer decision-making.
    • Concludes that a new approach is necessary for success in solution selling.

  • Chapter 2: The Challenger (Part 1): A New Model for High Performance

    • Introduces five distinct sales rep profiles, emphasizing the Challenger's success.
    • Describes how Challengers outperform in complex sales environments.
    • Stresses the Challenger's ability to teach and control sales conversations.

  • Chapter 3: The Challenger (Part 2): Exporting the Model to the Core

    • Focuses on the development of a Challenger sales force through training.
    • Details the importance of comprehensive organizational support.
    • Encourages businesses to invest in training, coaching, and tools.

  • Chapter 4: Teaching for Differentiation (Part 1): Why Insight Matters

    • Examines the value of customer insight in solution selling.
    • Introduces the concept of Commercial Teaching as a teaching method.

  • Chapter 5: Teaching for Differentiation (Part 2): How to Build Insight-Led Conversations

    • Outlines a six-step process for crafting effective customer conversations.
    • Teaches how to reframe customer challenges and quantify impact.

  • Chapter 6: Tailoring for Resonance

    • Discusses tailoring messages for different stakeholders.
    • Encourages understanding individual outcomes for effective communication.

  • Chapter 7: Taking Control of the Sale

    • Highlights the importance of taking control throughout the sales process.
    • Provides techniques for managing negotiations and handling tension.

  • Chapter 8: The Manager and the Challenger Selling Model

    • Details the pivotal role of sales managers in supporting the Challenger model.
    • Emphasizes the necessity for manager training in coaching and innovation.

  • Chapter 9: Implementation Lessons from the Early Adopters

    • Offers guidance on implementing the Challenger Selling Model effectively.
    • Stresses the importance of piloting and refining the model before a full rollout.
