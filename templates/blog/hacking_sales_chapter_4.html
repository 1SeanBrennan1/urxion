Chapter 4: Contact Information

Main Concepts

This chapter focuses on the essential task of finding accurate contact information for your leads. It stresses the importance of removing duplicates from lists using tools like Google Sheets add-ons to avoid wasted effort and resources. Additionally, it presents a variety of tools and techniques for finding and verifying email addresses and phone numbers.

  • Removing duplicate entries from lists is essential to avoid wasted effort.
  • Tools like Google Sheets add-ons can automate duplicate removal.
  • Utilize various tools and services to find accurate contact information (email, phone).

Key Concept

Accurate contact information (email addresses and phone numbers) is vital. Without it, even the best-targeted lists are useless. This chapter focuses on tools and techniques to find and verify contact details efficiently.

Implementation Plan

Practical Application 1: Remove Duplicates from Your Lists

Actionable Task 1.1: Consolidate and Deduplicate with Google Sheets

Steps:

  1. Gather Your Lists: If you've created lists using different databases or tools, combine them into a single Google Sheet.
  2. Install "Remove Duplicates" Add-on: Go to Add-ons > Get add-ons and search for "Remove Duplicates."
  3. Combine Sheets: Consolidate all your lists onto one master sheet within the Google Sheet.
  4. Run the Add-on: Select the entire data range, go to Add-ons > Remove Duplicates > Find duplicate or unique rows.
  5. Choose Your Method:
    • Delete Duplicates: If you want to keep only unique entries, choose "Remove duplicates" and select the columns to compare.
    • Highlight Duplicates: If you want to review duplicates before deleting, choose "Highlight duplicates" to mark them in green. Then manually remove them from one of the lists.

Resources: Google account, internet access.

Obstacles and Solutions:

  • Obstacle: Difficulty understanding Google Sheets - Solution: Consult Google Sheets tutorials online or ask for help from someone with spreadsheet experience.

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Actionable Task 1.2: Check Your CRM for Existing Accounts

Steps:

  1. Export CRM Data: Export a list of company names or contact information from your CRM.
  2. Compare with Master List: Add the exported CRM data to a new sheet in your Google Sheet document.
  3. Remove Duplicates: Use the "Remove Duplicates" add-on to compare the CRM data with your master prospecting list and remove any duplicates. This will prevent you from reaching out to prospects you're already in contact with.

Resources: Your CRM, Google Sheets.

Obstacles and Solutions:

  • Obstacle: CRM export limitations or complexity - Solution: Consult your CRM's documentation or support team for guidance on exporting data effectively.

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Practical Application 2: Find and Verify Email Addresses

Actionable Task 2.1: Use Email Finding Tools (Bulk Verification)

Steps:

  1. Choose a Tool: Consider Toofr, Hunter.io, or FindThatLead.
  2. Prepare Your Data: Make sure your prospect data includes first name, last name, and company domain.
  3. Upload Your List: Upload your list to the chosen email finding tool (most have a .csv upload feature).
  4. Download Verified Emails: The tool will process your list and provide a file with verified email addresses.

Resources: Chosen email finding tool (free trials or paid plans available), prepared prospect list (.csv format).

Obstacles and Solutions:

  • Obstacle: Cost - Solution: Use free trials strategically or choose a tool with a pricing model that aligns with your budget.

Timeline: Varies depending on the size of your list and the speed of the chosen tool (typically a few hours to a day).

Actionable Task 2.2: Verify and Enrich Data

Steps:

  1. Choose a Verification Tool: Use BriteVerify or NeverBounce to ensure email addresses are valid and reduce bounce rates.
  2. Enrichment: Consider using Clearbit to automatically enrich your data with additional information about the prospects (job titles, social media profiles, company details).

Resources: Chosen email verification and enrichment tools (free trials and paid plans are usually available).

Obstacles and Solutions:

  • Obstacle: Integration challenges - Solution: Many tools offer API integrations with popular CRMs or marketing automation platforms.

Timeline: A few hours (depending on the size of your list)

Evaluation Criteria

  • Deduplication: Have you successfully removed all duplicate entries from your lists?
  • Email Finding: What percentage of your list was successfully verified? How accurate are the found email addresses?
  • Data Enrichment: Have you gathered additional valuable information about your prospects?

Additional Resources

  • Email Hunter: A free tool to find email addresses associated with a domain.
  • FindThatLead: Another popular email finding and verification tool.

Now you have accurate contact information, which is essential for making a connection. Ready to move on to the next chapter!
