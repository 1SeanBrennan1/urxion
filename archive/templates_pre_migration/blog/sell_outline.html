Sell

  • Overview: "Sell" presents a comprehensive approach to personal selling, emphasizing trust-based relationships and long-term customer value.
  • Evolution of Selling: Guides readers through the shift from transactional to strategic, customer-centric selling focused on collaboration.
  • Trust Building: Explores key aspects of earning trust, including expertise, dependability, candor, customer orientation, and compatibility.
  • Understanding Buyers: Provides insights into the business buyer's journey, their needs, motivations, and decision-making processes.
  • Essential Skills: Equips readers with vital communication skills like active listening, strategic questioning, and effective verbal/nonverbal cues.
  • Sales Dialogue Framework: Outlines a practical framework for planning sales dialogues and presentations to ensure a structured approach.
  • Handling Objections: Addresses overcoming resistance and earning commitment with proven techniques to build buyer confidence.
  • Post-Sale Follow-Up: Emphasizes the importance of follow-up, collaborative involvement, and exceptional customer service in fostering long-term relationships.
  • Self-Leadership & Teamwork: Champions reflective self-leadership and teamwork as essential components for maximizing sales productivity and personal growth.

Chapters

  • Chapter 1: Overview of Personal Selling

    • This chapter introduces the fundamentals of personal selling, highlighting the shift from transactional selling to a more customer-centric approach.
    • It explores the diverse contributions of personal selling to society and businesses.
    • The chapter examines various personal selling approaches and the trust-based sales process.
    • Provides an overview of sales careers and the skills necessary for success.

  • Chapter 2: Building Trust and Sales Ethics

    • Focuses on the crucial role of trust in personal selling and its impact on customer relationships.
    • Highlights the importance of ethical behavior and maintaining trust with clients.
    • Addresses strategies for demonstrating expertise, dependability, and customer orientation.

  • Chapter 3: Understanding Buyers

    • Provides insights into the characteristics and needs of business buyers.
    • Distinguishes between consumer and business markets and their respective complexities.
    • Details the stages of the business buying process and how to influence each stage.

  • Chapter 4: Communication Skills

    • Emphasizes the role of effective communication in building buyer relationships.
    • Focuses on active listening and various questioning techniques for effective conversations.
    • Discusses the importance of nonverbal communication and body language.

  • Chapter 5: Strategic Prospecting and Preparing for Sales Dialogue

    • Defines strategic prospecting as a way to identify and prioritize sales opportunities.
    • Highlights the processes involved in generating, qualifying, and prioritizing leads.
    • Emphasizes the need for a prospecting plan and gathering information about prospects.

  • Chapter 6: Planning Sales Dialogues and Presentations

    • Stresses customer-focused planning for effective sales communications.
    • Introduces a sales dialogue template for organizing sales discussions.
    • Explores various formats for sales communication and their effectiveness.

  • Chapter 7: Sales Dialogue: Creating and Communicating Value

    • Discusses the characteristics of successful sales dialogues that create customer value.
    • Emphasizes buyer feedback and engagement techniques during sales conversations.
    • Covers the strategic use of sales aids and effective group dialogues.

  • Chapter 8: Addressing Concerns and Earning Commitment

    • Describes techniques for handling sales resistance and addressing buyer objections.
    • Presents the LAARC method for managing objections effectively and positively.
    • Discusses relationship-building strategies for earning commitment and closing sales.

  • Chapter 9: Expanding Customer Relationships

    • Focuses on post-sale activities to convert new customers into loyal advocates.
    • Highlights the importance of follow-up and addressing customer satisfaction.
    • Covers strategies for handling complaints and avoiding negative experiences.

  • Chapter 10: Adding Value: Self-Leadership and Teamwork

    • Emphasizes the role of self-leadership and teamwork in achieving sales success.
    • Covers the stages of effective self-leadership for sales professionals.
    • Discusses the power of teamwork within organizations to achieve shared goals.
